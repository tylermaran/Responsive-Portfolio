About Me

Graduated from Georgia State University with a B.B.A. in Economics and a focus in Finance. Working as a financial analyst for CIBC US in middle-market commercial banking. I assess credit proposals using quantitative and qualitative analysis through business and financial risk based metrics, making key recommendations in the form of approval, denial, limit adjustment or taking of security;

I am a level II candidate in the CFA program. A Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) is a professional designation given by the CFA Institute, formerly AIMR, that measures the competence and integrity of financial analysts. Candidates are required to pass three levels of exams covering areas such as accounting, economics, ethics, money management and security analysis.
